About Me

Hi, I’m Sophie Elsner, a 3D environment artist passionate about crafting captivating virtual worlds with Unreal Engine 5 and Blender.

My background in architecture taught me how to work efficiently, solve complex problems creatively, and stay on schedule - all while continuously learning new skills.

As a self-taught digital artist, I’ve embraced these principles to refine my craft, driven by curiosity, creativity, and a dedication to immersive design. I enjoy transforming imaginative ideas into stunning visual experiences, from richly detailed environments to seamless looping animations and dynamic concept art.
