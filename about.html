About Me

Hi, I’m Sophie Elsner, a 3D environment artist with a passion for creating captivating virtual worlds using Unreal Engine 5 and Blender.

My background in architecture taught me to work efficiently, solve complex problems creatively, and stay on schedule - all while continuously teaching myself new skills.

As a self-taught digital artist, I’ve carried these principles into my work and honed my craft through curiosity, creativity, and striving for immersive design. I love turning imaginative ideas into stunning visual experiences, from richly detailed environments to seamless looping animations and dynamic concept art.
